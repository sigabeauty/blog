Experiences of How to Learn and Teach Others

2026-04-16

Learn, remeber, teach others, make speech is a key skill. Not only make you learn and grasp more, but also help you build your influence. We need to make it as a skill and make life-long practice.

3+1 Questions to Ask before Preparing

When meeting an new domain or an new project, ask 3+1 questions: WHAT, WHY, HOW, WHO(if you’ll make speech and teach others).

  • WHAT: The definition of the thing you research, what is it, what problem it will solve, what’s the position it in current research. That’s all background information everyone needs to know.
  • WHY: Why is about some questions you ask after you know it, like “why it’s so famous?”, “why it can work?”, “why we need this?”, maybe we need to list some point to prove your point of view.
  • HOW: How to use it. If it is a problem or a tool, that’s the detailed manual of how to use it, maybe give some use cases.
  • WHO: Who to teach and talk is important while you need to teach the issue to others and make ohters learn and accept your opinion. When make materials, think about it to choose what to write and what to say.

Template while You are Making Notes and Slides.

1. Context & Positioning & Background (introduction first part in the paper)

The Intention of this part is let your listener (or yourself) know where we are and where we’ll go. Align the basic background and situation.

Landscape:

  • The macro field this topic belongs to, and some basic and necessary background knowledge of this field.
  • The current status and pain points.

Definition:

  • Use one simple sentence to make yourself and others know what is it.

Value Proposition:

  • What domain problem it solved.
  • Competitive analysis.

2. Architecture & Static global View (introduction second part in the paper or the start of second part paragraph)

This part is the framework of your issue.

3. Logic view & Detailed

Serval issues you can detailed of this topic. This is the Most important part and content.

4. Implementation & Parctice

Pracitice use, use some examples to tell your listener or yourself how to use it.

5. Critique & Future

Raise up the lession and show your expert point of view. Limitations, Roadmap, Conclusion.

The first 2 parts can use vedios, pictures and charts to show. Part 3 use more logic system like charts, part 4 more practical use cases.
